# Initial Concept
Moonshine Coffee Management Sim is a simulation game for managing coffee shop inventory and supply chain operations, currently migrating to a robust Laravel and React architecture.

# Product Definition: Moonshine Coffee Management Sim

## Vision
A high-stakes, professional tycoon-style game where players build a coffee empire through rigorous supply chain management and strategic optimization. The game balances the charm of coffee culture with the intense pressure of corporate logistics, challenging players to remain profitable while navigating a chaotic, unpredictable simulation.

## Target Audience
- **Casual Tycoon Fans:** Players who enjoy the "tycoon" genre and building a business from the ground up in a professional yet accessible setting.

## Primary Goals
- **Financial Success:** Maximize margins and cash flow to scale operations from a single stand to a global network.
- **Operational Excellence:** Master inventory logistics to minimize waste and eliminate stockouts.
- **Brand Reputation:** Cultivate trust through consistent quality and reliable vendor relationships.

## Core Features
- **Interactive Command Center:** A comprehensive real-time dashboard for monitoring financial health, inventory trends, and active simulation events.
- **Hybrid Event-Topology System:** A graph-based logistics engine that simulates physical routes and causal event chains, requiring players to navigate complex supply disruptions.
- **Chaos Engine (Spike Events):** A dynamic event system that introduces random disruptions—such as heatwaves or delivery strikes—requiring rapid tactical adaptation.
- **Mission & Progression System:** Structured challenges and contracts that unlock new locations, advanced upgrades, and higher-tier logistics capabilities.

## Gameplay Mechanics
- **Hybrid Management:** A flexible system allowing for both granular micromanagement of orders and high-level, policy-driven automation.
- **Strategic Policy Setting:** Define auto-replenishment rules and safety stock buffers to automate recurring logistics tasks.

## Visual and Atmospheric Style
- **High-Stakes Professionalism:** A sleek, analytical aesthetic that captures the fast-paced nature of corporate decision-making and logistics management.
